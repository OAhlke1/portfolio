  • I'm always interested in learning new things, because our technology continues to evolve. Ich bin stehts daran interessiert, neue Dinge zu erlernen, da sich unsere Technologie immer weiter entwickelt.
  • When I try to solve a problem, I first look for the most efficient approach.
    If implementation shows that my idea works, the next step is to consider how to implement the code as leanly as possible.
